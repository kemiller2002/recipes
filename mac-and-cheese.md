# Pro Mac & Cheese (Sloppy-Joe-Proof Edition)

A rich, savory, adult mac & cheese designed to stand up to beefy, tangy sloppy joes without getting lost. Creamy interior, structured sauce, crisp top.

---

## Why This Works
- Savory, not sweet (critical next to sloppy joes)
- Strong cheese backbone with excellent melt
- Crunchy topping for texture contrast
- Rich enough to feel indulgent, restrained enough to eat alongside a sandwich

---

## Ingredients

### Pasta
- 1 lb cavatappi or shells
- Kosher salt (for pasta water)

### Cheese Blend (by weight)
- 8 oz sharp cheddar (Tillamook or Cabot preferred)
- 4 oz Gruyère
- 4 oz Fontina
- 2 oz Parmigiano-Reggiano (reserve for topping)

### Sauce
- 4 tbsp unsalted butter
- 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 2½ cups whole milk
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- ½ tsp white pepper
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- ¼ tsp dry mustard

### Sloppy-Joe Compatibility Upgrade (choose one)
- 1–2 tsp Worcestershire sauce  
- OR ½ tsp MSG  
- OR a pinch of cayenne

### Topping
- ¾ cup panko breadcrumbs
- 2 tbsp melted butter
- Reserved Parmigiano-Reggiano
- Optional: minced parsley or chives

---

## Instructions

### 1. Cook the Pasta
- Bring a large pot of well-salted water to a boil.
- Cook pasta **1 minute shy of al dente**.
- Drain and set aside.

---

### 2. Make the Cheese Sauce
1. Melt butter over medium heat.
2. Whisk in flour and cook for 2 minutes (do not brown).
3. Slowly whisk in milk and cream.
4. Simmer until thick enough to coat a spoon.
5. Season with salt, white pepper, smoked paprika, and dry mustard.
6. Remove from heat.
7. Fold in cheddar, Gruyère, and Fontina until smooth.
8. Add chosen savory upgrade (Worcestershire, MSG, or cayenne).

---

### 3. Assemble
- Fold pasta into sauce.
- Transfer to a buttered baking dish or cast-iron skillet.
- Toss panko with melted butter.
- Top mac with reserved Parmesan, then panko.

---

### 4. Bake
- Bake at **375°F (190°C)** for 20–25 minutes.
- Broil for 1–2 minutes until golden and crisp.

---

## Optional Variations (Use One, Not All)
- Swap Fontina for smoked Gouda
- Fold in caramelized shallots
- Use bacon fat in breadcrumb butter (breadcrumbs only, no bacon chunks)

---

## Make-Ahead Notes
- Assemble fully, cover, and refrigerate up to 24 hours.
- Bake covered for first 15 minutes if cold, then uncover and finish.

---

## Serving Note
Serve hot, with sloppy joes on the side. Expect requests for seconds.
