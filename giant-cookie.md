# 9×9 Cookie Bar (or Giant Cookie)

If you're deciding between a giant cookie and a 9×9 cookie bar, they're essentially the same thing—the main difference is thickness and baking time.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, melted
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 cup chocolate chips

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to **350°F (175°C)**.
2. Grease a 9×9-inch baking pan or line it with parchment paper.
3. In a mixing bowl, combine the melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar.
4. Stir in the egg and vanilla until well combined.
5. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until just combined.
6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
7. Press the dough evenly into the prepared pan.
8. Bake for **22–28 minutes**, until the edges are golden brown and the center is just set.

## For a Gooey Center

Remove from the oven after **22–24 minutes**. The cookie bar will continue to cook slightly as it cools.

## For a Giant Cookie Cake

Use the same dough and bake it in a round cake pan or cast-iron skillet. Allow it to cool completely before decorating with frosting.

## Smoker Variation

If you're already using a smoker, place the cast-iron skillet on the smoker and cook indirectly at **325–350°F** for **25–35 minutes**. This adds a subtle wood-fired flavor.
