Ingredients
2 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup unsalted butter
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 large eggs
3 tablespoons water
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
* All ingredients should be room temperature 


Directions
1. Place oven rack in center of oven. 
2. Pre convection oven to 350 degrees F. 
3. In a mixing bowl, beat butter until creamy.
4. Add brown sugar to butter.
5. Add white sugar to butter. 
6. Beat butter and sugar mixture until fluffy. 
7. Scape down sides of bowl. 
8. Add eggs, vanilla, water, and slowly combine (low speed on a mixer). 
9. Whisk together flour, salt, and baking soda.
10. Combine flour mixture with wet ingredients.
11. Add chocolate chips and slowly combine.
12. Flip baking sheet over and line with foil.
13. Place 6 1 oz. cookie balls on upside down baking sheet and place in oven. 
14. At 8 1/2 minutes rotate the pan in the oven. 
15. At 17 minutes remove cookies from over. 
16. Let cookies cool for 5 minutes. 

Makes 22 cookies. 

Takes about an hour to make.
