# Spanish Chicken Noodle Soup (Sopa de Pollo con Fideos)

**Serves:** 6–8

A comforting chicken noodle soup with Spanish flavors from smoked paprika, tomatoes, olive oil, and optional saffron.

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 medium onion, finely diced
- 1 carrot, diced
- 1 celery stalk, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp sweet smoked paprika
- ¼ tsp hot smoked paprika (optional)
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- 8 cups chicken stock
- 2 cups cooked shredded chicken
- 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- Pinch saffron threads (optional)
- 6 oz fideos, vermicelli, or thin spaghetti broken into 1-inch pieces
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- Lemon wedges, for serving

## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a Dutch oven or large soup pot over medium heat.

2. Add the onion, carrot, and celery. Cook for 6–8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened.

3. Add the garlic, sweet smoked paprika, and hot smoked paprika (if using). Cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.

4. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 1 minute.

5. Add the diced tomatoes and cook for 2–3 minutes.

6. Pour in the chicken stock and add the saffron threads, if using.

7. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes.

8. Add the chickpeas and shredded chicken. Simmer for 5 minutes.

9. Add the noodles and cook until tender, about 5–8 minutes.

10. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

11. Stir in the parsley and serve with lemon wedges.

## Optional Spanish Tavern Variation

Add:

- 4 oz Spanish chorizo, diced
- 1 roasted red pepper, diced
- ¼ cup sliced green olives

### Method

1. Cook the chorizo first in the pot until lightly browned.
2. Remove excess grease if desired.
3. Continue with the recipe as written.
4. Add the roasted red pepper and olives with the chicken and chickpeas.

## Serving Suggestions

- Crusty bread rubbed with garlic and olive oil
- Fresh lemon wedges
- Extra parsley
- A drizzle of good-quality olive oil
- Additional smoked paprika for garnish

## Notes

- Fideos are traditional, but vermicelli or broken spaghetti work well.
- Rotisserie chicken is an excellent shortcut.
- The soup will thicken as the noodles absorb broth. Add additional stock when reheating if desired.
- Saffron is optional but adds a classic Spanish flavor and golden color.
