Cacao del Sol Competition Sauce

Main Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp neutral oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp dark molasses
  • 1 chipotle pepper in adobo
  • 1 roasted poblano pepper
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 oz 72% dark chocolate
  • 1 tbsp crushed cacao nibs
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp allspice
  • 1/4 tsp annatto
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp fresh orange juice
  • 1/2 tsp orange zest

Steps

  1. Cook onion slowly until deeply caramelized.
  2. Add garlic and cook briefly.
  3. Stir in tomato paste and cook until dark brick-red.
  4. Add all ingredients except chocolate and cacao nibs.
  5. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  6. Add chocolate and cacao nibs.
  7. Simmer 10 minutes.
  8. Blend until completely smooth.
  9. Pass through a fine-mesh strainer.
  10. Refrigerate overnight before judging.

Notes

  • Designed to showcase cacao without tasting like dessert.
  • Flavor progression: sweet, smoky, savory, bittersweet.
  • Best served at room temperature for judging.
