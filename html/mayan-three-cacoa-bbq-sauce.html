Mayan Three-Cacao BBQ Sauce

Main Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp agave nectar
  • 2 tbsp molasses
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 oz 70% dark chocolate
  • 1 tbsp crushed cacao nibs
  • 1 chipotle pepper in adobo, minced
  • 1 roasted poblano pepper, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/4 tsp annatto
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp orange juice
  • 1/2 tsp orange zest

Steps

  1. Heat oil and sauté onion until caramelized.
  2. Add garlic and tomato paste; cook until darkened.
  3. Add remaining ingredients except chocolate and cacao nibs.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Add chocolate and cacao nibs.
  6. Simmer 10 additional minutes.
  7. Blend thoroughly.
  8. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve.
  9. Chill overnight before serving.

Notes

  • Features cocoa powder, chocolate, and cacao nibs.
  • Orange enhances cacao flavor without becoming identifiable.
  • Intended as a more refined, competition-style sauce.
