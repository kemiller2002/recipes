Moroccan-Inspired Short Rib Compendium

This compendium collects several Moroccan-inspired directions for braised beef short ribs. Each concept shares the same foundation: deeply browned short ribs, slow braising, and a sauce reduced until glossy.

1. Classic Moroccan Braised Short Ribs

Flavor Profile

  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • Turmeric
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Beef stock
  • Preserved lemon
  • Green olives
  • Fresh parsley and cilantro
  • Toasted almonds

Serve With

  • Saffron couscous
  • Preserved lemon mashed potatoes
  • Parsnip purée

2. Date & Pomegranate Short Ribs

Flavor Profile

  • Dates
  • Pomegranate molasses
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Cumin
  • Beef stock

Finish

  • Fresh pomegranate arils
  • Toasted pistachios
  • Mint

Character

Rich, savory, and lightly sweet with bright acidity.

3. Ras el Hanout Short Ribs

Homemade Blend

  • Coriander
  • Cumin
  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • White pepper
  • Black pepper
  • Turmeric
  • Nutmeg
  • Allspice
  • Cardamom
  • Pinch cloves
  • Optional smoked paprika

Serve With

  • Pearl couscous
  • Roasted carrots
  • Charred onions

4. Harissa-Glazed Short Ribs

Method

Braise traditionally.

Reduce the braising liquid.

Finish with:

  • Harissa
  • Honey
  • Butter

Brush onto the ribs and caramelize under a broiler before serving.

5. Apricot & Almond Tagine Style

Flavor Profile

  • Dried apricots
  • Toasted almonds
  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • Saffron
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Character

The apricots melt into the sauce, creating a naturally silky texture.

Moroccan Building Blocks

Citrus

  • Preserved lemon

Herbs

  • Cilantro
  • Parsley
  • Mint

Nuts

  • Marcona almonds
  • Pistachios
  • Pine nuts

Warm Spices

  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • Turmeric
  • Cardamom
  • Allspice
  • Clove

Side Dishes

  • Saffron couscous
  • Toasted almond couscous
  • Parsnip purée
  • Whipped sweet potatoes with preserved lemon
  • Roasted cumin-honey carrots
  • Charred eggplant with yogurt

Sauce Finish

Reduce the braising liquid until it coats a spoon.

Finish with:

  • Cold butter
  • Fresh herbs
  • Preserved lemon
  • Splash of sherry vinegar or lemon juice

Signature Concept

A restaurant-style presentation featuring:

  • Ras el Hanout braised short ribs
  • Glossy preserved lemon pan sauce
  • Saffron parsnip purée
  • Roasted rainbow carrots
  • Crispy chickpeas
  • Toasted Marcona almonds
  • Cilantro, parsley, and mint
  • Fresh pomegranate seeds

The result is unmistakably short ribs first, with Moroccan flavors unfolding in layers rather than overwhelming the beef.
