# Red-Chile Base ("Spell Component Paste")

## Yield
Approximately 1½ cups

## Ingredients

- 6 guajillo chiles
- 3 ancho chiles
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1½ tsp ground cumin
- 1½ tsp Mexican oregano
- 2–3 tbsp neutral oil
- Hot water
- Chicken stock or soaking liquid

---

## Method

### 1. Prep

Remove stems and seeds from chiles.

### 2. Toast

Toast briefly in a dry skillet until fragrant.

Do not burn.

### 3. Soak

Cover with hot water.

Soak 10–20 minutes.

Reserve soaking liquid.

### 4. Blend

Blend:

- softened chiles
- garlic
- cumin
- oregano
- oil
- soaking liquid

Blend until completely smooth.

### 5. Strain

Pass through a fine mesh strainer.

---

## Usage

### Chicken
Use approximately ½ cup.

### Beef
Use approximately ¼–⅓ cup.

### Queso
Use 1–2 teaspoons maximum.

# Griffon Shreds
## Red-Chile Braised Chicken

### Yield
4 lb chicken

---

## Dry Brine

### Ingredients

- 4 lb chicken thighs
- 26 g kosher salt
- 1 tsp sugar (optional)
- ½ tsp baking powder (optional)

### Method

Mix thoroughly.

Refrigerate:

- Best: 12–24 hours
- Good: 6 hours
- Minimum: 90 minutes

---

## Braise

### Ingredients

- Dry-brined chicken
- ½ cup red-chile base
- 2½ cups chicken stock
- Neutral oil

---

### Method

#### Sear

Sear chicken aggressively.

#### Braise

Add chile base.

Add stock halfway up the chicken.

Cover tightly.

Bake:

325°F

45–55 minutes

---

### Finish

Rest 10 minutes.

Pull by hand.

Reduce braising liquid.

Fold chicken back in.

Add:

- 2–3 tbsp butter or chicken fat
- lime zest
- a little lime juice
- 1–2 tsp apple cider vinegar

---

### Hold

150–160°F

Covered.

# Ironhold Mince
## Service-Stable Nacho Beef

### Yield
4 lb beef

---

## Ingredients

- 4 lb 85/15 ground beef
- 24–25 g kosher salt
- 1 tsp baking powder (optional)
- 3–4 tbsp beef tallow (preferred)
  OR
- 3 tbsp neutral oil

### Aromatics

- 1 medium onion, brunoise
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp Mexican oregano

### Seasonings

- 1½ tsp cumin
- 1½ tsp coriander
- ¾ tsp white pepper

### Liquids

- ¾–1 cup beef stock
- 1–2 tsp ACV or lime juice
- ¾–1 tsp MSG (optional)

### Chile Base

- ¼–⅓ cup

---

## Method

### Season

Combine:

- beef
- salt
- baking powder
- white pepper

Rest 15–30 minutes.

---

### Aromatic Base

Sweat:

- onion
- garlic
- oregano

Reserve.

---

### Brown

High heat.

Add beef in chunks.

Smash once.

Brown thoroughly.

Break into crumbles.

Add:

- cumin
- coriander

Fold aromatics back in.

---

### Steam

Add chile base.

Add stock.

Cover 3–5 minutes.

Uncover.

Reduce until glossy.

---

### Finish

Add:

- MSG (optional)
- ACV or lime

Adjust salt.

---

### Hold

150–155°F

Covered.

# Molten Gold of the Deep
## Upgraded Queso

### Ingredients

### Base

- 1 large can Rico's Nacho Cheese Sauce

### Upgrade

- 1½–2 cups whole milk
- 2–6 oz white American cheese
- ½ tsp white pepper
- ½–1 tsp MSG
- 1 onion wedge
- 1 garlic clove
- 1–2 roasted poblanos
- 1–2 tsp lime juice

---

## Method

### Infused Milk

Warm milk with:

- onion
- garlic

Steep 5–7 minutes.

Strain.

---

### Build

Warm queso.

Whisk in infused milk.

Add:

- white pepper
- MSG
- poblano puree

If needed:

add American cheese.

---

### Finish

Add tiny amount lime juice.

---

### Hold

145–155°F

Stir every 20–30 minutes.

Use hot milk if thickening.

# Quick Pickled Red Onions
## Alchemist's Preserves

### Ingredients

- 1 large red onion
- ½ cup apple cider vinegar
- ½ cup water
- 1½ tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp sugar

Optional:

- peppercorns
- bay leaf

---

## Method

Slice onions thin.

Bring vinegar mixture to boil.

Pour over onions.

Rest:

- 30 minutes minimum
- 1 hour preferred

Drain lightly before serving.

# Forge-Kettle Mac
## Service-Stable Mac & Cheese Base

### Ingredients

#### Pasta

- 2 lb cavatappi

#### Sauce

- 6 cups whole milk
- 2 cups light cream
- 6 tbsp butter
- 6 tbsp flour

#### Cheese Blend

- 10 oz white American
- 6 oz sharp white cheddar
- 3 oz Gruyère or Fontina
- 2 oz Parmesan (optional)

#### Seasoning

- White pepper
- Salt
- Optional Dijon

---

## Method

Cook pasta just under al dente.

Build béchamel.

Melt cheeses gently.

Fold pasta into sauce just before service.

---

## Hold

150–155°F

Keep hot milk available.
