# Spanish-Inspired Rib Dinner Menu

A Spanish-style dinner menu centered on smoked paprika ribs with tapas, sides, dessert, and beverage pairings.

## Main Course

### Spanish Smoked Paprika Ribs with Sherry-Honey Glaze

#### Rib Rub
- 2 tbsp smoked paprika (pimentón)
- 1 tsp sweet paprika
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp cumin
- ½ tsp chili flakes
- 1 tsp brown sugar (optional)

#### Sherry-Honey Glaze
- ½ cup dry sherry (Fino or Amontillado)
- 1½ tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp sherry vinegar
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, grated
- Pinch smoked paprika
- Pinch black pepper

#### Finishing Garnish
- Chopped parsley
- Lemon zest
- Flaky sea salt
- Good olive oil

## Sides

### Garlic Roasted Peppers (Pimientos Asados)

#### Ingredients
- 3–4 red bell peppers
- 2–3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, sliced
- 1 tbsp sherry vinegar
- Pinch smoked paprika
- Salt
- Chopped parsley

#### Method
1. Roast peppers until skins blacken.
2. Steam in a covered bowl for 10 minutes.
3. Peel and slice.
4. Warm olive oil and garlic together.
5. Toss peppers with garlic oil, vinegar, paprika, salt, and parsley.
6. Serve at room temperature.

### Padrón (or Shishito) Peppers
- Blister in olive oil.
- Finish with flaky salt and lemon.

### Citrus Fennel Salad

### Spanish Roasted Potatoes

Roast until crisp and golden.
