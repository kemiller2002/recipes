# Sweet Home Chichén Itzá Sauce

## Main Recipe

### Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 cup fire-roasted tomatoes
- 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1 tbsp molasses
- 1 tbsp cocoa powder
- 1 oz dark chocolate
- 1 chipotle pepper in adobo
- 1 roasted poblano pepper
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp annatto
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp orange juice
- 1/2 tsp orange zest

### Steps

1. Sauté onion until softened and lightly browned.
2. Add garlic and tomato paste and cook until fragrant.
3. Add remaining ingredients.
4. Simmer for 30 minutes.
5. Blend until smooth.
6. Strain if desired.
7. Chill overnight before serving.

---

## Notes

- Sits between BBQ sauce, mole, and table sauce.
- Fire-roasted tomatoes add depth and smoke.
- Orange and cacao create a distinctly Yucatán-inspired profile.
- Designed for originality while remaining approachable.
