# Mayan Night BBQ Sauce

## Main Recipe

### Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 small onion, finely diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 cup tomato sauce
- 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 cup honey
- 2 tbsp molasses
- 1 tbsp cocoa powder (Dutch process preferred)
- 1 oz 70% dark chocolate, chopped
- 1 chipotle pepper in adobo, minced
- 1 tsp adobo sauce
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp allspice
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 cup water

### Steps

1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Sauté onion until softened and lightly caramelized.
3. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
4. Stir in tomato paste and cook until darkened.
5. Add remaining ingredients.
6. Simmer for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
7. Blend until smooth.
8. Refrigerate overnight before serving.

---

## Notes

- The cocoa powder provides bitterness and roasted notes.
- The dark chocolate softens acidity and adds richness.
- Excellent with pork, chicken, or beef.
